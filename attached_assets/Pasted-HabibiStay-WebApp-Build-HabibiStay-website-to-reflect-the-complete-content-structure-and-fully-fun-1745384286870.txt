Functional Requirements
Update the website to include the following functionalities, organized into modular plugins for seamless integration with the existing codebase.
1. Core Booking & Property Functions
* Property Listings & Search:
    * Display property details (images, descriptions, amenities, pricing, location, availability).
    * Enable search and filtering by location, price range, dates, property type, etc.
    * Include an interactive map view to visualize property locations.
    * Highlight "Featured" listings and special promotions.
* Booking Process:
    * Implement a multi-step booking workflow:
        * Real-time availability calendar.
        * Collect guest details (name, contact, special requests).
        * Process payments securely in SAR, supporting refunds and modifications.
        * Issue instant booking confirmations via email/SMS.
        * Allow booking modifications and cancellations.
* Reviews & Ratings:
    * Enable guests to leave reviews and star ratings.
    * Allow hosts to respond to reviews.
    * Display aggregate ratings on property pages.
2. User Roles & Account Functions
* Guest/Traveler:
    * Registration/login with email and social options.
    * Profile management (details, password, preferences).
    * View booking history and manage reservations.
    * Save favorite properties (wishlists).
    * Submit reviews and ratings.
* Host:
    * Register as a property owner.
    * Create and manage property listings (details, images, availability).
    * Set pricing rules and booking policies.
    * View earnings and financial reports.
    * Communicate with guests via messaging.
* Employee:
    * Manage properties on behalf of hosts.
    * Coordinate maintenance and guest communications.
    * Access a dashboard for operational tasks.
* Investor:
    * Register as an investor.
    * View portfolio details (ROI, occupancy rates, revenue trends).
    * Download financial reports (PDF/CSV).
    * Receive notifications about investment opportunities.
* Admin:
    * Oversee system and manage content.
    * Approve/edit/delete user accounts, listings, and reviews.
    * Configure site settings (payment, language, currency).
    * Access analytics and financial reports.
    * Use a drag-and-drop page builder for frontend edits.
    * Monitor audit logs and security events.
* AI Bot (Sara):
    * Virtual sales assistant (no login required).
    * Greet visitors and assist with accommodation needs.
    * Recommend properties and upsell investment opportunities.
    * Guide users through booking and log interactions.
3. AI-Powered Chatbot Functions (Sara)
* Conversational Interface:
    * Initiate chats on homepage and booking pages.
    * Understand inquiries in real-time, enabling users to view, select, book, and pay within the interface.
    * Respond in Arabic and English based on user preference.
    * Suggest properties and answer FAQs.
* Sales & Upselling:
    * Proactively suggest investment options and add-on services (e.g., tours, maintenance packages).
* Configuration & Logging:
    * Allow admins to configure prompts and log conversations for review.
4. Advanced Admin Management & Content Editing
* Drag-and-Drop Page Builder:
    * Visual editor for creating/editing pages and templates with live preview.
    * Support reusable templates.
* Custom Content Management:
    * Edit static pages (About Us, Contact, FAQ) and blog posts.
    * Manage dynamic content (chatbot prompts, emails) with version control.
* Dashboard & Reporting:
    * Real-time monitoring (bookings, revenue, user growth).
    * Custom financial reports (earnings, commissions, ROI) exportable to CSV/PDF.
* Role & Permission Configuration:
    * Define granular permissions and customizable access controls.
    * Maintain audit logs for admin actions.
5. Payment & Financial Functions
* Payment Processing:
    * Process bookings in SAR with refund and partial payment support.
* Financial Reporting:
    * Generate host revenue summaries.
    * Provide investor dashboards (ROI, occupancy, trends).
    * Schedule automated report delivery.
6. Localization, Currency, & Translation Functions
* Multi-Language Support:
    * Dynamic translation for Arabic and English with a toggle.
    * Support RTL layouts.
* Currency Handling:
    * Default to SAR with proper formatting.
    * Prepare for future multi-currency support.
7. Marketing, SEO, & Analytics Functions
* SEO Optimization:
    * Use clean URLs, meta-tags, and mobile-responsive design for fast load times.
* Referral & Promotion Systems:
    * Enable referral codes, discounts, and seasonal promotions.
* Analytics & Tracking:
    * Track user engagement and conversions with real-time dashboards.
8. Third-Party Integrations & Automation
* Social Media & Authentication:
    * Support social logins (e.g., Facebook, Google).
* Mapping & Location:
    * Display property locations on an interactive map.
* Email & Notifications:
    * Send transactional emails (confirmations, reminders).

Design Notes
* Color Scheme: Brand blue (#2957c3) for headers, buttons, and accents; white background with soft gray highlights.
* Typography: Clean, modern fonts suitable for English and Arabic (e.g., Arabic-friendly options).
* Imagery: High-quality photos of properties, guests, and the team.
* Responsiveness: Fully mobile-friendly with fast load times.
